Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place a trade?

To place a trade, navigate to the Trade page, select your desired stock, choose buy or sell, enter the quantity and price, then click "Execute Trade". The system will process your order immediately.

How often do stock prices update?

Stock prices on our platform update every 10 seconds to provide you with near real-time market data for informed trading decisions.

Can I cancel a trade after placing it?

Once a trade is executed, it cannot be canceled. All trades are processed immediately upon submission. Please review your order details carefully before executing.

How do I view my portfolio performance?

Visit the Portfolio page to see your current holdings, market values, profit/loss calculations, and overall portfolio performance. The data updates automatically with current market prices.

Where can I see my trade history?

The History page contains a complete record of all your trades, including buy and sell orders, quantities, prices, and timestamps. You can use this to track your trading activity over time.

Is there a minimum trade amount?

The minimum trade quantity is 1 share. There are no minimum dollar amounts, but you must have sufficient funds to complete the transaction.

How secure is my account?

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted passwords, secure session management, and role-based access controls to protect your account and trading data.

Can I access my account from mobile devices?

Yes! Our platform is fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices. You can access all features from your smartphone or tablet.
