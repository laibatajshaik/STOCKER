About Stocker

Stocker is a cutting-edge stock trading platform designed for both novice and experienced traders. Our platform provides real-time market data, instant trade execution, and comprehensive portfolio management tools.

Our Mission

To democratize stock trading by providing accessible, powerful, and secure trading tools that empower users to make informed investment decisions.

Technology Stack

  • Backend: Python Flask with robust authentication
  • Database: SQLite for local deployment, DynamoDB for AWS
  • Real-time Updates: WebSocket-based price streaming
  • Security: Session-based authentication with role management
  • Notifications: Email and AWS SNS integration
